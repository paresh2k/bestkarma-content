## Content Production Workflow

```
STAGE 1: RESEARCH (done for all 52 articles — research/ files)
    ↓
STAGE 2: DRAFT BRIEF (done for 35 articles — drafts/ directories)
    ↓
STAGE 3: FULL ARTICLE DRAFT
    Author writes 1,200-1,800 words using draft brief
    + AI assistance for prose/structure
    + All citations formatted as inline links
    ↓
STAGE 4: SCIENCE VALIDATION
    Each claim traced to primary source
    No overclaiming beyond what evidence supports
    Hedging language where evidence is preliminary
    ↓
STAGE 5: EDITORIAL REVIEW
    Tone: empowering but honest, not alarmist
    Structure: hook → evidence → mechanism → practical
    Reading level: smart layperson, not clinician
    ↓
STAGE 6: PUBLISH
    Move to published/articles/ in bestkarma-content repo
    Push → GitHub Action triggers Vercel deploy hook
    Article live within 5 minutes
```

**Goal velocity:** 1 article/week minimum, 2 articles/week at full pace

---

## Quality Standards

1. **Every factual claim cites a primary source** (peer-reviewed study or systematic review preferred; major health organization guidelines accepted)
2. **No diet culture language** — no "detox," "clean eating," "guilt," "cheat day"
3. **Statistical literacy** — never report relative risk without base rate context; distinguish association from causation
4. **Honest uncertainty** — "preliminary evidence suggests" vs. "studies show" vs. "established fact"
5. **Practical close** — every article ends with specific, actionable steps graded by effort level
