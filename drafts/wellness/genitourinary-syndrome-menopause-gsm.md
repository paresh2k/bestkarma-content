## Brief

Cover what GSM is (the combined vaginal, urological, and sexual symptoms of estrogen deficiency), why it worsens without treatment (unlike vasomotor symptoms), the treatment options from least to most systemic (vaginal moisturisers, local estrogen, ospemifene, systemic HRT), the safety evidence for local estrogen in breast cancer survivors, and the shame and silence problem.
