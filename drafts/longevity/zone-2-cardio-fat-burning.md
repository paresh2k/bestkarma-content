## Brief

Zone 2 training is low-to-moderate intensity aerobic exercise performed at a heart rate where you can hold a conversation but would struggle to sing. It's the training zone where:

- Fat oxidation is maximised (primary fuel source)
- Mitochondrial biogenesis is highest (new mitochondria created)
- Lactate is produced and cleared in balance (metabolic efficiency)

## Key Angles

- Why most people train at the wrong intensity (too hard for Zone 2, not hard enough for Zone 4/5)
- The Iñigo San Millán / Peter Attia connection — Zone 2 as the longevity cardio prescription
- Mitochondrial density as a longevity biomarker
- How to find YOUR Zone 2 (heart rate, talk test, lactate threshold)
- Minimum effective dose: 3 hours/week across 3-4 sessions
