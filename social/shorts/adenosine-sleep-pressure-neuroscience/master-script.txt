Hook: Caffeine doesn't give you energy; it just masks your sleep pressure.

Beats:
1. Adenosine builds up in your brain the longer you stay awake, creating a biological need for sleep.
2. When you consume caffeine, it blocks adenosine receptors, preventing you from feeling tired, but it doesn't stop adenosine production.
3. This leads to a 'caffeine crash' when the effects wear off, revealing the sleep debt you've accumulated.
